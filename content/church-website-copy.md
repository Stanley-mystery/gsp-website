WHO WE ARE

# The Good Shepherd's Pasture

The Good Shepherd's Pasture is a parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG). Guided by the overarching visual and foundational goal of RCCG globally, our goal is to make heaven and take as many people as possible with us.

"Our friends testify that we are a loving and vibrant multi-cultural church. We are blessed with over 2,000 worshippers at our centre every Sunday."

God's Special People, as we are fondly called, invites you to worship with us and partake in the Word in an atmosphere of God's presence.
